Welcome to 

Timber Panel

Timber Panel Products stocks and distributes a wide range of plywood, MDF and particle board products to the building, furniture and general manufacturing industries.

Established in 1984, Timber Panel Products has built a solid reputation for competitive pricing, an excellent range of quality products as well as superior service.

Specializing in timber sheet materials we look forward to your enquiry and hope we can be of service to you. These pages list our standard products stocked in a wide range of sizes, however we are only too happy to source your requirements.

Why choose 

Timber Panel?

With Australian Timbers and Timber Panel Products sharing so many attributes in the way they go to market, including the fact that many suppliers supply both companies, it was an obvious ‘fit’ for Australian Timbers to purchase Timber Panel Products. The acquisition of Timber Panel Products with its strong market position, has certainly given AT’s an excellent entrée into the Victorian market.

Timber Panel Products has a small but motivated, focused team of people that care about their customers.  The results prove that by listening to the customer, we have been instrumental in building solid relationships ensuring continued support for Timber Panel Products.  It is because we genuinely care about our customers’ needs, that our customers keep coming back for more.
